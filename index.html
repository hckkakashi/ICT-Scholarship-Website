Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

Check the criteria below to see if you qualify for the LBEF ICT Scholarship Program.

Nepalese citizen who has completed +2 / Higher Secondary Education (Science or Management stream preferred)

Minimum GPA of 2.8 or equivalent percentage (50% or above) in SEE and +2

Demonstrated interest or background in ICT, mathematics, or related technical subjects

Economically disadvantaged students from rural areas are especially encouraged to apply

Students who have not previously received a full scholarship from any other institution

Must pass the LBEF entrance assessment test and attend a personal interview

Age limit: 16–25 years old at the time of application submission

Must maintain a minimum CGPA of 3.0 each semester to retain the scholarship

Application Process

Four simple steps stand between you and your scholarship. Let's get started.

Fill Application

Complete the online scholarship application form with your academic and personal details.

Submit Documents

Upload your academic transcripts, citizenship, recommendation letter, and SOP.
